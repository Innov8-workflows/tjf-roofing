Work We Offer

The complete roofing service — leadwork, re-roofs, repairs, the lot.

🏠

Re-Roofs

Complete strip-and-replace re-roofs. New membrane, battens, tiles or slates. New ridge, new leadwork where needed. Built to last.

🧱

Flat Roofs

Flat roof installs and repairs on extensions, dormers, garages and bay windows. Properly drained, fully sealed, no nasty surprises.

🔧

Repairs & Leaks

Slipped tiles, broken slates, leaks that keep coming back, valley repairs. No job too small. Phone any time.

🏛️

Leadwork

Fresh lead flashings around chimneys, valleys, abutments and parapet walls. Heritage-grade lead, properly dressed to stay waterproof.

🧱

Chimneys & Ridging

Chimney repointing, flaunching, stack removal and rebuilds. Ridge re-bedding or dry-ridge conversions. Long-term fixes, not band-aids.

💧

Guttering, Fascias & Soffits

New uPVC guttering, fascias and soffits. Everything that keeps water off the walls and the roof line looking sharp.

🧱

Repointing on Brickwork

Traditional lime mortar or modern repointing on chimneys, gable ends, and parapet walls. Fixes damp problems at source.

💨

Vents & Ventilation

Roof vents, tile vents, soffit vents and ridge ventilation. Stop the condensation that rots rafters from the inside.

📐

Written Quotes

Every job quoted in writing, itemised, competitive. What we quote is what you pay. No surprises, no "found more issues" mid-job.
